Motivation

Poland started the Family 500+ program which provides family benefits in an effort to fight high levels of childhood poverty. This program provides families with 500PLN per month for each child after the first until the child’s 18th birthday. The program was announced in early-mid 2015 and went into effect April 2016. At the same time, Poland has been experiencing a declining birth rate for many years and the Family 500+ program hoped to encourage fertility. I decided to focus on what effects this program has had on the country’s birth rate.
